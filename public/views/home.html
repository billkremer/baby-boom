Baba Baby Boom is your go-to app for communicating to grandparents and other relatives.

Keep your family up-to-date with cute pictures and your baby's latest achievements. There's just four easy steps:


1. Start with the Milestones. Personalize your baby's milestones and save their achievements!



2. Review your baby's Achievements. Correct any missing or incorrect information and click to Update their info.



3. Everyone loves to see photos of your baby. Select your picture, add a comment, and click to Upload. Your photo of your darling baby will be added below!



4. We're ready to create an email. Select one to four recent achievements and a photo and click to Send. That's it!
